Tourist Places in Hyderabad

Charminar

The symbol of Hyderabad, Charminar was built in 1591 and remains a monument of Indo-Islamic architecture.

Golconda Fort

A majestic fortress known for its amazing acoustics and historical significance.

Hussain Sagar Lake

A heart-shaped lake famous for the giant Buddha statue standing in the middle of it.

Secretariat

One of the administrative office of the employees of the Government of Telangana in India

Birla Mandir

A beautiful white marble temple offering a panoramic view of Hyderabad city.
